PIZZA


"Seven Days Without Pizza Makes One Weak"
Ingrediants
  • 600ml warm water

  • 1 teaspoon sugar

  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast

  • 7 cups all purpose flour

  • 6 tablespoons olive oil

  • 1.5 teaspoons salt

  • Tomato sauce

  • Mozzerella cheese

  • Required Toppings

Procedure
  1. Mix yeast and sugar in warm water and
    let sit for 10 minutes

  2. Combine flour and salt in a large bowl,
    make a well in the center and add oil
    and the bloomed yeast mixture while
    mixing with a spoon.

  3. Transfer the dough into a clean work
    surface and knead for 10-15 minutes until
    its soft and bouncy.

  4. Grease a bowl, place the dough inside
    and let rise for atleast an hour or a day

  5. After an hour, knead again and divide
    into 4 portions.

  6. Preheat the oven between 450-500 degree
    fahrenheit.

  7. Using a portion of the dough, flatten it
    and put on the tomato sauce, cheese nad the
    required toppings.

  8. Bake the pizza for about 15 minutes until
    the crust is brown and the cheese is melted.
