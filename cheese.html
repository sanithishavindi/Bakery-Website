CHEESE BISCUIT


"Mind Your Own Biscuits"
Ingrediants
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour

  • 3 teaspoons baking powder

  • 1 tablespoon sugar

  • 1 teaspoon salt

  • 3/4 teaspoon cream of tartar

  • 1/2 cup cold butter

  • 1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese

  • 1 garlic clove, minced

  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper
    flakes

  • 1-1/4 cups milk

Procedure
  1. Preheat oven to 450Â°. In a large
    bowl, whisk flour, baking powder,
    sugar, salt and cream of tartar.

  2. Cut in butter until mixture resembles
    coarse crumbs.

  3. Stir in cheese, garlic and pepper flakes.

  4. Add milk; stir just until moistened.

  5. Drop dough by heaping 1/4 cupfuls 2 in.
    apart onto a greased baking sheet.

  6. Bake 18-20 minutes or until
    golden brown. Serve warm.
