SHORTBREAD COOKIES


"A Balanced Diet Is a Cookie in Each Hand"
Ingrediants
  • 142g of room temperature
    butter

  • 1/2 cup of sugar

  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

  • 1 1/2 cups of flour

  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Procedure
  1. Beat butter and vanilla until creamy.

  2. Add sugar and salt and mix until
    combined.

  3. Scrape bowl down and add flour while
    breating on low until the whole
    mixture is combined.

  4. Shape the dough into a rectangular prism,
    wrap in plastic and chill for about
    an hour.

  5. Preheat oven to 177C.

  6. Use a sharp knife to cut 1/2 inch thick
    slices.

  7. Place the cookie slices on a baking sheet
    and use a fork to make a pattern.

  8. Bake the oookies for about 10minutes and
    transfer to a wire sheet to
    cool down.
