HASHBROWNS


"No hashtags, Only Hashbrowns"
Ingrediants
  • 2 medium russet potatoes,
    shredded

  • ½ medium onion, finely
    chopped

  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour

  • 1 egg

  • 1 cup oil for frying, or
    as needed

  • salt and pepper to taste

Procedure
  1. Rinse shredded potatoes until
    water is clear, then drain and
    squeeze dry. Place shreds in a
    bowl,and mix in the onion, flour
    and egg until evenly distributed.

  2. Heat about 1/4 inch of oil in
    a large heavy skillet over medium
    high heat.

  3. When oil is sizzling hot, place
    potatoes into the pan in a 1/2 inch
    thick layer. Cover the whole bottom
    of the pan, or make separate piles like
    pancakes. .

  4. Cook until nicely browned on the
    bottom, then flip over and brown on
    the other side. It should take at least
    5 minutes per side. If you are cooking
    them in one big piece, it can be cut into
    quarters for easier flipping.

  5. Remove from pan, and drain on paper
    towels. Season with salt and pepper and
    serve immediately
