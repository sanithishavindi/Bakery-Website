DOUGHNUTS


"Everything is Better With Doughnuts"
Ingrediants
  • Milk 190g

  • Dry yeast 7g

  • Sugar 35g

  • Salt 7g

  • 1 egg

  • Flour 350g

  • 40g butter

Procedure
  1. Mix milk and yeast together

  2. Add sugar and salt into the mixture and mix.

  3. Add the egg and mix well.

  4. Add the flour and mix until a dough is formed.

  5. Knead lightly and leave for 15 mins.

  6. After leaving, add the butter and knead for 5 -7mins.

  7. After butter is absorbed, bang the dough
    and knead for about 5-7mins until smooth.

  8. Make a ball and leave it in a bowl for about 1hour

  9. Make a ball and leave it in a bowl for
    about 1hour.

  10. Make small balls and leave for 15 mins.

  11. Make the donut shapes and keep on a well
    surface away from each other for about 1hr.

  12. Heat oil and fry on medium heat
